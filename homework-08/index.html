How to Turn a Computer Off and On Again

Home About Contact
  1. Identify that there is a problem with your computer

  2. Turn the Computer Off
    • Idealy, go to the menu bar of your computer and press shutdown
    • However if your computer is frozen, hold down on the Power Button

  3. Your Computer is likely shutdown but here are a few things to look out for
    • You want to make sure there are no lights that are still on
    • However, some computers will have lights indicating that it is charging but the computer is off.
      So make sure that it is plugged in if you're are getting charging lights

  4. Now, it is time to press the power button on your computer to turn it back on

There is a 95% chance that your computer is now working
