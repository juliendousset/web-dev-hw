Crepes

(For 2 to 3 People)

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup of buckwheat flour
  • ¼ cup of potato starch + 1 egg OR ½ cup of potato starch
  • Optional: 2 tbsp of sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • ¼ cup of (canola or other) oil
  • 2 cups of water

Directions

  1. Mix the first 4 ingredients together.
  2. Mix in the oil, then the water slowly so as to avoid getting clumps.
    Alternatively, use a large blender to mix all the ingredients.
  3. Heat up a flat pan on medium heat then pour the batter in the pan,
    tilting the pan so as to make sure it spread across the pan.
  4. When the top of the crepe is dry,
    flip the crepe over and cook the other side for a minute at most.
